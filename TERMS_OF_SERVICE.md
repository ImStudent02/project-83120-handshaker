## 3. Privacy First Approach

### What We Collect (Minimal)

- Username (chosen by you)
- Encrypted email (for account recovery only)
- Birthday (for key derivation, stored locally)
- PGP public key (for key exchange)

### What We DO NOT Collect

- Message content (never passes through our servers)
- Private encryption keys
- Contact lists
- Location data
- Usage patterns

### What We Cannot See (Everything is Encrypted)

- Your messages (AES-256 encrypted, P2P only)
- Your files (AES-256 encrypted, P2P transfer)
- Your private keys (stored on your device only)
- Any communication between users (we have zero access)
